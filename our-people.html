CO-CREATOR

Born in Colva, south of Goa. Maria Suzette grew up in a Portuguese colonial house. She is the eldest amongst four siblings. Earlier times home food was devoured, besides that there was no other food placed on to the table. Our home food is nutritious and healthy. She completed her schooling and went to pursue her five years degree in fine art. Then she founded her own advertising agency with Rony Floriano and functioned for a decade at the beginning of her career.

“Those early days, we realised the importance of home healthy food”. We realised the need to retain the same home cooked cultures. To add to that Goa was loosing her culinary heritage, being a world visitors destination there was no one sketching Goa’s cuisine in a credible way. With an artist instinct she dedicate her talent and started in the year 1997, Mum’s Kitchen – Ethnic Cuisine Movement.

Today this movement is alive and she is content for Goa’s culinary voice is heard in many countries. For her the on going continual campaign of ethnic movement is a testimony what an artist can bring in lasting impressions on a culinary canvas. Indeed Mum’s Kitchen art for her is inspiring and priceless.

Maria Suzette
CO-CREATOR

Given an option to Rony Floriano to duplicate any existing setup or create one unique model he will settle for the latter one by indigenously creating from the grass root and finishing to the last bit in designing an organisation.

Mum’s Kitchen is one model uniquely created different in the gourmet category. His management structures are optimal, with minimal control span, linear hierarchy in form of reverse pyramid.We call Mum’s Kitchen, and even in our brand guidelines (SOP) stating not to call a restaurant instead Mum’s Kitchen, the ethnic cuisine movement.

He steers the brand, in a 360 degree. His eye for detail is incapable, so as his skill in specialised process controls inventory management, Human Resources, consistency in food operations and creating a home cooking process model instead of other restaurants duplicated model.

His learning came in from being as advertising incumbent holding additional degree in Applied Arts. In addition to this he is holding a degree from premiere India’s business school, Goa Institute of Management (GIM). He has to his credit state awards and also undergone training in food safety in UKAS programme.He was awarded Goa’s best restaurateur award from the Times of India Group. He continues to lead as a mentor and a visionary.

Rony Floriano
MANAGER

“If you chase passion and education most likely you’ll end up choosing your career fired with passion”. Yes this humble woman Akshara Gaokar, hails from Shirgao, North Goa, has lived it up to what passion can do to anyone today.

This gourmet aficionado celebrates Goan food everyday. Akshara joined us in the year 2012 as a Food Safety Executive. She majored in microbiology and handles CCP (Critical Control Point) , SSOP (Sanitization Standard Operating Procedures) as mandated by International Organization for Standardization (ISO) through our accredited certifying body (CB) world leader in testing, inspection and certification Bureau Veritas.

Over the years she has meticulously scaled up the ladder displaying strong resilience and is leading Mum’s Kitchen in a position of manager of our ethnic cuisine movement.

Akshara Gaokar
COMMUNITY PRODUCE COORDINATOR

Franzine Nunes
KITCHEN HANDLER

We relocated Vilasni Gaude from our home as domestic help to Mum’s Kitchen after spotting her talents in cooking at a very young age. She joined Mum’s Kitchen in 2006 and rolled over in all departments.

Finally she moved up in her early years from stewarding to handling kitchen. She proved her mettle as a women, and she is a single earner to the family. Like others for her too this becomes her first and last place of work. Cooking for her is love and not a commercial activity.

Vilasni Gaude
