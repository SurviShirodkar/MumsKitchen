LETTER FROM OUR CO-CREATOR

"DEV BAREM KORU"

When we started Mum’s Kitchen two and half decades ago, never realised we would reach at what we are today. We have a wide range of authentic Goan hindu and catholic home dishes.These recipesare curated to every specification for colour, texture and taste. Thesedishes have made their way on our menu. This is an arduous task but the work to bring back the lost food of Goa indeed is satisfying and the journey is fulfilling. Akshara, Franzine, Vilasni and Rony are most precious to me andwho never came from any culinary educationand so are, our other dedicated team.

Our approach is different in working with women. We employ mothersfrom different villages. They are our stakeholders. They cook better than trained professionals. They kept motivating us to move ahead. We have deep respect for Olga Aires, our mother who has left implacable legacy, by the way she picked up spices and cooked food. We thank families, giving encouragement to our employees, dedicated to Mum’s Kitchen. For most of them this is their first and the last job. Our salute to our vendors who are doing a commendable job, for we are very fussy and stringent quality performers, rejecting produces which does not conform to standards.

Finally we have to say “Gracias”,”Merci”,”Gracie”,”Arigato”,”Do Jen”“Danke Sehr“,“Shukran”, Thank – You and finally “Dev Borem Korum”, for all our world over well wishers and guest. This is a small place, with only ten tables. We are grateful to you all, without you this would have not been possible, hoping to see you one day.

Maria Suzette
